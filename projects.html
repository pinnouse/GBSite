Sgnow

Description

Sgnow (pronounced 'snow') is a quick and efficient music player written in Rust and NodeJS.

Purpose

Create a beautiful music player that's customizable and extensible with a small memory footprint. Of course the irony is using electron for the frontend, but there are no native alternatives otherwise that offer such a high degree of accessibility.
